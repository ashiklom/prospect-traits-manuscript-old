6 Supplementary information

Figure 6.1: Comparison of parameter estimates across PROSPECT versions. p4 = PROSPECT 4, p5 = PROSPECT 5, p5b = PROSPECT 5B.

Agreement between models is strongly parameter dependent. The most consistent agreement was for the water (Cw) and dry matter content (Cm) parameters. Agreement between PROSPECT 4 and 5 was substantially better than agreement of either model with PROSPECT 5B.

Overall, PROSPECT 5B had the best inversion accuracy, with the highest R2 values for all parameters except dry matter content (“Cm”). By parameter, the best inversion accuracy was achieved for leaf water content (“Cw”), with all remaining parameters having roughly comparable, poor inversion accuracy.
